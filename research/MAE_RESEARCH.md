### TOTAL DX+ (gut detox, flagship)
- "Only Moringa-added premium detox product in Malaysia; high fibre, high enzyme, high chlorophyll." Removes GI-tract waste, claims blood purification and hormonal-balance help.

- Use: 1 sachet in 200ml room/cold water, immediately; 1–2/day; best before bed (body detoxes during sleep; night-shift workers take before their morning sleep).
- Not a laxative; no cramping (distinguish mild pre-bowel-movement signal from cramps). Severe constipation: daily → taper to alternate days; moderate 3–4x/wk; mild 1–2x/wk. Bloating at start = fiber adaptation → start with ½ sachet. Fewer bowel movements after a while = normal. Can substitute for late-night snacks (fiber expands = satiety).
- Pregnant: only after month 4. Breastfeeding: OK in moderation, start ½ sachet, wait 2–3 weeks postpartum. Kids: not <3y; 3–6y ¼ pack; 6–12y ½; ≥12y full. After alcohol: wait 1h. Sick with fever: skip until recovered. Helps menstrual hormone regulation/cramps (their claim).

4. **XP/M-COIN math** as closing sweetener (show coins earned at their tier).
